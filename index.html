About Us

Welcome to the CTC-to-Basic Pay Calculator! We specialize in helping individuals and organizations better understand how salary structures work, making it easy to calculate Basic Pay, House Rent Allowance (HRA), Special Allowance, and Deductions from the total Cost to Company (CTC).

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly and accurate tool to break down CTC components so that employees can clearly see their payable salary. Whether you're in India, the USA, or elsewhere, our calculator adjusts to your region's salary structure.

Our platform is perfect for professionals, HR departments, and financial enthusiasts seeking clarity in salary breakdowns. We aim to simplify complex salary terms and ensure you understand what your CTC translates to in terms of real earnings.
